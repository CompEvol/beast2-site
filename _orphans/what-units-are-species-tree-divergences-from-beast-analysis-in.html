Let $r_i$ be the estimated relative rate for locus $i$ and let $L_i$ be the number of sites in locus $i$. If *BEAST is run with the mean substitution rate across partitions fixed to 1, and the clock rate fixed to 1, then the following is true:

<math> \frac{\sum_i r_i L_i}{\sum_i L_i} = 1, </math>
